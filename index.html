ABOUT US

Welcome to Hot Beans Web, your gateway to future technology. As an up-and-coming tech company, we're on a mission to redefine digital landscapes.

Hot Beans Web, Founded by John Smith in 2019, is a small to medium Web Design & Development Company based in Angel, England.

We are a dynamic team of digital enthusiasts, committed to the world of tech. We blend nnovation with ambition, crafting solutions that will propel our buisness into the new era of success.

Since 2019, Hot Beans Web has been teaching web designers, and at the moment, the company is seeking to hire more web developers.

First Opening of Hot Beans was revealed in Angel, London, and we've established a reputation for creating aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly websites over the past few years.

"If future generations are to remember us more with gratitude than sorrow, we must achieve more than just the miracles of technology. We must also leave them a glimpse of the world as it was created, not just as it looked when we got through with it."

John Smith, Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
